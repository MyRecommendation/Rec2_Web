Our Story

It's All About the People

My Recommendation was built on a simple belief — great experiences come from great people, not just great places. We created a platform where communities can recognize and share the talented individuals who make their lives better every day.

Get the App

Why We Built My Recommendation

Have you ever tried to find a great hair stylist or nail technician and realized it's nearly impossible? You search online, read generic reviews, and still end up gambling on a stranger. The truth is, the best professionals are found through word of mouth — through people who actually know and trust them.

That's the gap we set out to close. My Recommendation is a community-driven app where real people recommend the service professionals they genuinely love. Whether it's a yoga instructor who changed your life, a bartender who makes every event a hit, or a personal trainer who helped you reach your goals — the REC app gives them the recognition they deserve.

See How It Works

What We Stand For

Three principles guide everything we build

Community First

We believe trust is built person to person. Every recommendation on REC comes from a real community member who has experienced that professional firsthand — no paid reviews, no bots.

Recognition Matters

Great work deserves recognition. Our Bronze, Silver, and Gold status system ensures the best professionals get the visibility and opportunities they've earned through genuine excellence.

Empowering Professionals

We give service providers the tools to own their story — a profile, photos, social links, and a schedule — so they can grow their business on their own terms.

Our Mission

My Recommendation exists to bridge the gap between talented service professionals and the people who need them most. We want every great yoga instructor, bartender, personal trainer, and nail technician to have a platform that showcases who they truly are.

For the people searching, we want to make finding a trusted professional as easy as asking a friend. For the professionals building a career, we want to give them a community that grows with them.

We're not just an app — we're a movement to recognize and celebrate the people who make everyday experiences extraordinary.
